**Prompt1**: create a painting that uses watercolor and emphasizes movement, transparency, gradients, unity and watery layering. use small brushes and soft colors and the wet-on-wet technique for a soft, blended background. The composition should feature a sunset on the beach with a woman sitting on the sand looking at it.
**Prompt2**: create a monotype artwork that emphasizes movement, looser painterly gestures, gradients, and unity. use water-soluble soft inks and the additive (painting/rolling ink) technique for a soft, blended background. The composition should feature a sunset on the beach with a woman sitting on the sand looking at it.
